Our Story

We are a team of designers and engineers that want to bring the same level of usability, reliability, and affordability to fixed-wing drones that we have seen in quadcopters. With this goal in mind, we have developed a new drone that excels at large-scale aerial surveying and brings new innovations to fixed-wing drones that will propel us to a new standard.
